EchoWave is a social media platform that generates a positive and “green” environment targeting sustainability in mental and physical health. Our goal is to educate teenagers (ages 13 and up) to learn about sustainable facts, do dancing challenges, and have fun!

OUR APP

  • Challenge yourself to improve your physical health through daily dance challenges. Each day, EchoWave presents a new routine to keep you moving and motivated.
  • Did you know that small changes in your daily routine can have a big impact on our planet? Learn a new sustainability fact every day with EchoWave's "Do you know" feature, and take on fun challenges to make a difference.
  • At EchoWave, we believe in creating a positive social environment that supports mental wellness. That's why we've included a filter to keep negative language out of our platform, so you can focus on connecting with others in a clean and green space.

Our Missions/Inspirations

In today's world, social media has become an integral part of our lives. It has changed the way we communicate, interact, and share information. However, as social media continues to evolve, it is crucial that we also take steps to ensure it is a friendly, "green," and clean environment for teenagers. The younger generation is the future, and social media can be a powerful tool in inspiring them to create a positive impact on the world. By using social media to share stories of environmental activism, sustainability, and other green initiatives, we can motivate and encourage young people to get involved and take action. We must also consider the impact of social media on mental health and wellbeing. While social media has the potential to connect us with others and offer support, it can also be a source of anxiety, stress, and negative self-image. By promoting positive and healthy online interactions and encouraging self-care, we can create a safer and more nurturing environment for teenagers. In short, social media is a double-edged sword, and it's up to us to make it a force for good. By prioritizing a green, clean, and friendly environment, we can inspire the young generation to become positive agents of change and create a better future for all.
